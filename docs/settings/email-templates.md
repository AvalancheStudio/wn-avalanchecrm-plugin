# Email Templates

Customise the communication your clients and staff receive from the CRM using the Email Templates module.

## Template Management

- **Dynamic Placeholders**: Use variables like `{{ client.name }}` or `{{ invoice.number }}` to personalise automated emails.
- **Custom HTML & Styling**: Full control over the layout and design of your emails to ensure they match your brand.
- **Trigger-Based Mailing**: Configure specific events (like a new ticket or an overdue invoice) to trigger automated emails.
- **Preview & Test**: Send test emails to yourself to see exactly how your communications will appear to your users.
