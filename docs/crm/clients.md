# Clients

Manage your customer relationships effectively with the comprehensive Client Management module. Avalanche CRM allows you to maintain detailed records and history for every client.

## Managing Clients

- **Client Profiles**: Store essential information including company details, primary contacts, and billing addresses.
- **User Association**: Link Winter CMS backend or frontend users to specific client accounts for portal access.
- **Activity History**: Automatically track all interactions, from profile updates to invoice payments.
- **Notes & Attachments**: Keep internal notes and relevant documents attached directly to the client profile.

## Client Portal

If enabled, clients can log in to their own portal to view their projects, download invoices, and manage support tickets.
