# Dashboard

The Avalanche CRM Dashboard provides a high-level overview of your business operations. It serves as the central hub where you can quickly monitor key metrics and stay updated on recent activities.

## Key Features

- **Performance Overview**: Visualise your revenue, active subscriptions, and pending invoices at a glance.
- **Recent Activities**: A feed of the latest updates across clients, projects, and tickets.
- **Quick Actions**: Shortcuts to common tasks like creating a new client, project, or invoice.
- **Statistics**: Real-time data on active projects, open support tickets, and monthly growth.

## Customisation

The dashboard can be tailored to show the most relevant information for your team, ensuring that the most critical data is always front and centre.
