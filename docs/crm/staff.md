# Staff

The Staff module allows you to manage your team members and their roles within the CRM system.

## Staff Management

- **Team Directory**: Maintain a list of all staff members with their contact details and assigned roles.
- **Role Assignment**: Define what each team member can access and manage within the CRM.
- **Project Allocation**: Assign staff members to specific projects and tasks to track their workload.
- **Performance Tracking**: Monitor staff contributions across various projects and support tickets.
