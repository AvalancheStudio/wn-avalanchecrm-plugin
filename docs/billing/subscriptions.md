# Subscriptions

Streamline your recurring revenue with the Subscriptions module, designed to handle ongoing service billing automatically.

## Subscription Management

- **Plans & Tiers**: Create various subscription plans with different pricing and billing cycles (monthly, yearly, etc.).
- **Automatic Renewal**: Subscriptions automatically generate invoices and process payments based on the set frequency.
- **Grace Periods**: Configurable settings for handling failed payments and subscription lapses.
- **Client Self-Service**: Allow clients to view, upgrade, or cancel their subscriptions through the portal.
