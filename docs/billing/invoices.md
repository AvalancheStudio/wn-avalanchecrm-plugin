# Invoices

Avalanche CRM includes a powerful billing engine that automates the invoicing process and helps you get paid faster.

## Invoicing Capabilities

- **Professional Templates**: Generate clean, branded PDF invoices automatically.
- **Tax & Currency**: Support for multiple tax rates and global currencies to suit international business.
- **Status Monitoring**: Track invoice states from Draft and Sent to Paid and Overdue.
- **Online Payments**: Integrate with popular gateways like Stripe, PayPal, and GoCardless for seamless client payments.
- **Partial Payments**: Record and track split payments against a single invoice.
