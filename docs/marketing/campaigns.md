# Campaigns

The Marketing module allows you to create and manage campaigns to reach your clients effectively.

## Campaign Features

- **Targeted Messaging**: Send newsletters, announcements, or promotional offers to specific client segments.
- **Template Builder**: Use predefined email templates or create custom designs for your campaigns.
- **Scheduling**: Plan your communication strategy by scheduling campaigns for future dates.
- **Engagement Tracking**: Monitor open rates, click-through rates, and overall campaign effectiveness.
