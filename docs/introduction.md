# Introduction

Avalanche CRM is a powerful, comprehensive Customer Relationship Management (CRM) and Billing system designed exclusively for Winter CMS. It aims to streamline your business operations by providing a centralized platform for client management, project tracking, invoicing, support tickets, and recurring subscriptions directly within your CMS backend.

## Why Avalanche CRM?

Whether you're an agency, freelancer, or a growing business, managing clients across multiple disparate systems can be challenging. Avalanche CRM brings all these essential tools under one roof natively within your Winter CMS installation, giving you full control over your data and workflows.

## Core Capabilities

- **👥 Client Management**: Maintain detailed profiles, contact information, user associations, and activity logs for all your clients.
- **🏗️ Projects & Tasks**: Organize work with robust project management, assignable tasks, staff roles, and Kanban-style tracking.
- **🧾 Professional Invoicing**: Maintain your brand with automatic PDF invoice generation, curated tax calculations, built-in currency support, and tracking for outstanding and paid invoices.
- **🎟️ Support Tickets**: Provide excellent customer service with an integrated client-facing ticketing system featuring categories, priority levels, and status tracking.
- **🔄 Subscriptions & Billing**: Manage recurring billing for various plans and subscriptions, with seamless integration for Stripe, PayPal, and GoCardless.
- **🎨 Dynamic Theming**: Tailor the CRM dashboard and client portal to match your company's aesthetic using built-in CSS variables for dynamic customisation.

This documentation will guide you through the process of installing, configuring, and utilizing all the features that Avalanche CRM has to offer.
